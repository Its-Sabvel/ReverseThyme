About Me

Hi, my name is Emma. I am a licensed Esthetician with over 5 years of providing skincare and wellness services. My approach combines traditional techniques alongside holistic methods to help clients achieve optimal skin and physical well-being. My idea for my brand was to integrate the power of herbs, science-backed ingredients, and skin-care advice/products tailored to your skin. I understand that in the world of marketing and consumerism, that choosing products for your skin can be overwhelming, that's where I can help. I also understand frusturation, when you feel that you've tried everything and nothing seems to work on your skin. Living by the mountains has it's perks, but our skin says otherwise, I'll help you find the right products to counter dry winters. Lastly, my favorite part... but to find out, you'll have to book an appointment with me for a true, private spa experience.
